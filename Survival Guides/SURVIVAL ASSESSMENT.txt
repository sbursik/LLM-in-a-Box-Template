Below is a **print-ready Survival Assessment Checklist** formatted for **paper use**.
It is designed to be **clear, scannable, and actionable** during high-stress conditions.


---

# SURVIVAL ASSESSMENT CHECKLIST

**Government Collapse / Post-Apocalyptic Scenario**

**Name / Group:** ___________________________
**Date:** ___________________
**Location:** _______________________________

---

## 1. SITUATIONAL AWARENESS

☐ Nature of collapse identified (government / utilities / currency / supply chain)
☐ Scope understood (local / regional / national / global)
☐ Reliable information source identified
☐ Rumors verified or dismissed
☐ Immediate changes tracked (hourly/daily)
☐ Contingency assumptions documented

---

## 2. PERSONAL SAFETY

☐ Immediate threats identified
☐ Unsafe areas mapped
☐ Defensive posture established
☐ Visibility level chosen (low / moderate / high)
☐ Conflict avoidance strategy in place
☐ Escape plan prepared

---

## 3. SHELTER

☐ Primary shelter secured
☐ Secondary shelter identified
☐ Weather resistance adequate
☐ Fire risk assessed
☐ Concealment evaluated
☐ Entry points controlled
☐ Structural integrity verified

---

## 4. WATER (CRITICAL)

☐ Current water source identified
☐ Backup water sources located
☐ Purification method available
☐ Storage containers secured
☐ Daily water ration defined
☐ Long-term access plan established

---

## 5. FOOD & NUTRITION

☐ Food inventory completed
☐ Estimated duration calculated
☐ Preservation method available
☐ Acquisition skills identified (hunt/fish/forage)
☐ Cooking method secured
☐ Nutritional gaps identified

---

## 6. HEALTH & SANITATION

☐ First aid supplies inventoried
☐ Prescription needs identified
☐ Medical skills available in group
☐ Sanitation system established
☐ Waste disposal plan in place
☐ Disease prevention measures active

---

## 7. ENERGY & HEAT

☐ Cooking fuel secured
☐ Heating method available
☐ Lighting method available
☐ Renewable energy source identified
☐ Fuel storage secured
☐ Fire safety measures in place

---

## 8. TOOLS & EQUIPMENT

☐ Essential tools inventoried
☐ Repair capability assessed
☐ Spare parts available
☐ Manual alternatives identified
☐ Tools secured against theft
☐ Improvised solutions planned

---

## 9. MOBILITY & TRANSPORT

☐ Stay vs move criteria defined
☐ Routes mapped
☐ Navigation tools available
☐ Bug-out bag prepared
☐ Load weight manageable
☐ Rally points established

---

## 10. COMMUNITY & TRUST

☐ Group members vetted
☐ Roles and skills assigned
☐ Rules agreed upon
☐ Conflict resolution method defined
☐ Security responsibilities assigned
☐ Exit conditions defined

---

## 11. GOVERNANCE & ETHICS

☐ Decision authority defined
☐ Rules documented
☐ Accountability system in place
☐ Non-negotiable ethical boundaries defined
☐ Abuse prevention measures in place

---

## 12. TRADE & RESOURCES

☐ Barter goods identified
☐ Trade risks assessed
☐ Dependency risks identified
☐ Resource protection plan in place
☐ Trade boundaries defined

---

## 13. INFORMATION & KNOWLEDGE

☐ Critical knowledge preserved
☐ Paper records maintained
☐ Maps available
☐ Skills training ongoing
☐ Knowledge transfer plan for children

---

## 14. PSYCHOLOGICAL RESILIENCE

☐ Stress management strategies identified
☐ Daily routine established
☐ Morale indicators monitored
☐ Purpose and goals defined
☐ Mental health warning signs known

---

## 15. LONG-TERM SUSTAINABILITY

☐ Food production plan
☐ Water sustainability plan
☐ Shelter longevity assessed
☐ Skill development plan
☐ Rebuilding priorities defined

---

## 16. CHILDREN & FUTURE

☐ Child safety measures in place
☐ Education plan established
☐ Core values defined
☐ Cultural knowledge preserved
☐ Vision for future articulated

---

### FINAL STATUS CHECK

☐ Immediate survival secured (72 hours)
☐ Short-term stability achieved (30 days)
☐ Long-term survivability plausible (6+ months)
