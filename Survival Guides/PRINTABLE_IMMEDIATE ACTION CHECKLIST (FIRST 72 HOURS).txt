IMMEDIATE ACTION CHECKLIST (FIRST 72 HOURS)
Status: URGENT | Priority: SAFETY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

PHASE 1: THE FIRST 30 MINUTES
[ ] STOP moving for 30 seconds. Breathe. Assess.
[ ] 360-Degree Scan: Check for fire, structural damage, or immediate hostiles.
[ ] Locate all family/group members.
[ ] Move away from "Violence Zones" (crowds, windows, main roads).
[ ] Secure immediate shelter (Lock doors, blackout windows).

PHASE 2: WATER & UTILITIES (The First Hour)
[ ] Fill bathtubs and sinks with water immediately (before pressure fails).
[ ] Shut off gas line if you smell leaks or suspect structural damage.
[ ] Inventory bottled water. (Goal: 1 Gallon per person/day).
[ ] Locate water purification gear (Bleach/Filter/Boiling pot).

PHASE 3: SECURITY & COMMS
[ ] Lock and barricade external doors.
[ ] Cover windows (Blackout curtains or cardboard).
[ ] Establish a "Safe Room" (interior room, no windows).
[ ] Turn on Emergency Radio (NOAA/Local News).
[ ] Silence phones to save battery; text your status to out-of-area contact.

PHASE 4: INVENTORY
[ ] Count all food calories available.
[ ] Count all ammunition/defense tools.
[ ] Locate medical kit.
[ ] Assign roles: Watchman, Runner, Medic.

REMINDER: Panic kills. Focus on the next right step.
